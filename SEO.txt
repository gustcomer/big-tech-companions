================================================================================

Search Queries: words or phrases users type into a search engine like Google, Bing, or DuckDuckGo to find information, products, or services.
  Navigational Queries: queries where the user intends to find a specific website or page. "Facebook login", "YouTube".
  Informational Queries: queries where users seek general knowledge or information without a direct intent to purchase. "What is SEO?" "Best places to visit in Norway"
  Transactional Queries: queries where users show intent to take a specific action, such as making a purchase, signing up, or downloading something.
